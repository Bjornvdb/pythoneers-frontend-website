Who are we?

CoffeeTaste is a platform where you can find and order exotic coffee breweries and we deliver them to your home. We hand-pick the best coffee brews and offer them here after a thorough quality testing. Each month, new roasts will be added to our shop. After you’ve ordered a brew, you can receive a taste sampler and fill in a questionnaire to discover your coffee taste.
